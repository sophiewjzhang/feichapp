Chinese Word Board

Having difficulty remembering Chinese words? Learn and remember words by trying this app today!

The App provides an easy and fun way to learn for anyone to remember the fundamental Mandarin words. First start by taking a look at the word dictionary and then play a card matching game to help with memorization. It's perfect for anyone who wants to learn their Chinese vocabulary quickly!
